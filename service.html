How to Order

To order food, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website or mobile app.
  2. Browse the menu and select the items you want.
  3. Go to your cart and review your order.
  4. Proceed to checkout and provide your delivery details.
  5. Complete the payment process.
  6. Wait for your delicious meal to be delivered!

Delivery Options

We offer the following delivery options:

  • Standard Delivery: Arrives within 30-45 minutes.
  • Express Delivery: Arrives within 15-30 minutes (additional charges may apply).
  • Pickup: Order online and pick up your food from our nearest location.

Payment

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Card
  • Debit Card
  • Online Wallets (e.g., PayPal, Google Pay)
  • Cash on Delivery (available for select areas)
