How to Order

To order food, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website or mobile app.
  2. Browse the menu and select the items you want.
  3. Go to your cart and review your order.
  4. Proceed to checkout and provide your delivery details.
  5. Complete the payment process.
  6. Wait for your delicious meal to be delivered!

Delivery Options

Standard Delivery

Arrives within 30-45 minutes. Free for orders over $20.

Express Delivery

Arrives within 15-30 minutes (additional charges may apply).

Pickup

Order online and pick up your food from our nearest location.
